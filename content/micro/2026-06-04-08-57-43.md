A useful [asymptotic
approximation](https://www.johndcook.com/blog/2026/05/26/calculating-expected-normal-range/)
for the expected range of n samples drawn from a N(0, 1) random variable:

$$
d_n = 2\Phi^{-1}\left(\frac{n - 0.375}{n + 0.25}\right).
$$
