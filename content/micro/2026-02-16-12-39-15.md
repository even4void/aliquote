[An invitation to a sparse Cholesky factorisation](https://dansblog.netlify.app/posts/2022-03-23-getting-jax-to-love-sparse-matrices/getting-jax-to-love-sparse-matrices). Left-looking Cholesky factorization in two lines:

```python
for j in range(0,n):
  L[j,j] = sqrt(A[j,j] - L[j, 1:(j-1)] * L[j, 1:(j-1)]')
  L[(j+1):n, j] = (A[(j+1):n, j] - L[(j+1):n, 1:(j-1)] * L[j, 1:(j-1)]') / L[j,j]
```
